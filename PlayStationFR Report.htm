1. Introduction

One of the methods that were introduced in Modeling Techniques for Big Data course was text analysis. Through this project, we would like to implement this technique along with sentiment analysis using Twitter for business purposes. The goal of this project is to be able to gather information from social media, clean and transform the data, and classify it to help business create a sentiment market research tool.

 

In this project, we would like to capture people's emotions and sentiment relating to PlayStation France, for example, the tweets will be classified into positive and negative. The steps that will be taken in this project are as follows:

 

1. Installing necessary packages and connecting to Twitter API

2. Scraping Twitter data, both from the search bar and personal account of the business

2. Cleaning and transforming the acquired data

3. Plotting the data for analysis

3.1 Acquiring data from Twitter search feature

We use search_tweets function to mine 10,000 tweets that contain specific keywords "PlayStation OR Playstation OR playstation" from Twitter search feature. We limit the language to French only. The tweets can only be extracted from the past 6 to 9 days, therefore for the purpose of obtaining a reproducible example of the project, 10000 tweets were downloaded and exported to a CSV file.

tweets <- search_tweets("PlayStation OR Playstation OR playstation", n=10000,lang = "fr")

df = do.call("rbind", lapply(tweets, as.data.frame))

6. Conclusion

Analysis of the sentiments and emotions regarding the French branch of PlayStation using Data from Twitter shows that the company has a generally positive public reception. We see that anticipation, trust and joy are the main sentiments expressed whenever a tweet is targeted at them, which is mainly due to the multitude of exclusive games, deals and advantages regularly provided to its player base. With the recent release of its newest console, declared to be the largest launch in the company’s history, and the limited supply due to the global semiconductor shortage, Casual gamers and enthusiasts alike are hoping to get a chance to snatch one of these devices, a need that exacerbated due to the ongoing pandemic. Other entities see this as an advertising opportunity thus many raffles and prizes are offered online which is shown by the numerous words related that scored high within our Word Cloud.
